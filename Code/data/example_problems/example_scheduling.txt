Context

The hospital operates three daily shifts, each 8 hours long:

Morning: 7 AM – 3 PM

Evening: 3 PM – 11 PM

Night: 11 PM – 7 AM

1. Shift coverage:

Each shift must have:

1 Charge Nurse (experienced RN)

2 other RNs (at least 1 ICU-certified total)

2 LPNs

2 NAs

2. Work hour limits:

Maximum 40 hours/week per employee.

No consecutive night → morning shifts.

Minimum 12 hours rest between shifts.

No employee may work more than 6 consecutive days.

3. Fairness:

Distribute weekend shifts evenly among all non-restricted staff.

Avoid assigning all night shifts to the same few people (unless preferred).

Ensure that at least 3 nurses get 2 consecutive days off per week.

Fiona prefers night shifts.

Cara and Dana cannot work weekends.
